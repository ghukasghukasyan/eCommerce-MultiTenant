Section 01

Who Is This Platform For?

This eCommerce platform is designed for small to medium-sized businesses that want a professional, fully managed online store without building one from scratch. It is ideal for retail, fashion, cosmetics, lifestyle, and specialty goods brands.

Target Customer Profiles

🛍️

Retail Brand Owner

Has products to sell and needs a polished, mobile-ready online store launched quickly.

👗

Fashion & Lifestyle Brand

Needs a visual, editorial-style storefront with image galleries, hero banners, and variants (size, color).

📱

Influencer-Driven Business

Works with social media influencers and needs a built-in coupon & commission tracking system.

🏪

Multi-Brand Agency

Manages several store brands and needs a white-label solution deployable per client tenant.

🌍

International Seller

Sells across regions and needs multilingual storefront support out of the box.

🚀

Growing Startup

Needs a scalable, production-ready backend without the overhead of building custom infrastructure.

This Platform Is NOT For

  • Businesses needing a marketplace (multi-vendor selling by third parties)
  • Digital-only products (software licenses, ebooks) — designed for physical goods
  • Enterprise-scale operations requiring custom ERP/WMS integrations out of the box

Section 03

Storefront — Customer Experience

The storefront is what your customers see and interact with. It is designed to be visually compelling and frictionless — guiding shoppers from discovery to checkout in as few steps as possible.

Home Page

  • Hero carousel — Full-screen rotating banner with custom image, title, subtitle, tag, and call-to-action button per slide. Auto-advances every 5.5 seconds. Swipeable on mobile.
  • Trust strip — Configurable brand highlights (e.g. "Free Shipping", "Secure Payment", "Easy Returns") displayed below the hero.
  • Category showcase — Visual grid of product categories linking directly to the category listing page.
  • Best sellers — Auto-populated section showing the top 8 featured products.
  • Editorial banner — Full-width promotional banner with background image, headline, and CTA.

Product Listing (Category Page)

  • Grid layout with product cards showing image, name, price, and quick-add to cart
  • Image slider per card — multiple product images swipeable without opening the detail page
  • Sorting options (newest, price, etc.)
  • Pagination for large catalogs

Product Detail Page

  • Multi-image gallery with swipe gesture support
  • Thumbnail strip for quick image navigation
  • Fullscreen lightbox on image tap/click with pinch-to-zoom on mobile
  • Variant selection (e.g. size, color) before adding to cart
  • Breadcrumb navigation (Home → Category → Product)
  • Product description with expand/scroll on mobile

Shopping Cart

  • Persistent cart across sessions
  • Quantity adjustment per item
  • Variant display (shows selected attributes per item)
  • Coupon code input field
  • Real-time order total with discount applied

Search

Keyword search across all active products with live results.

Checkout & Payment

  1. 1Customer reviews cart and applies coupon (optional)
  2. 2Selects or adds a delivery address
  3. 3Proceeds to secure payment
  4. 4Redirected to success page on completion or cancellation page if abandoned
  5. 5Order is recorded and visible in their account

Section 05

Influencer & Coupon Program

The platform includes a complete influencer marketing system — one of its most powerful differentiators. You can recruit influencers, assign them unique coupon codes, set commission rates, and automatically track the performance of every code they share.

How It Works

  1. 1Admin creates an influencer — enters name, social media handles, and status (active/inactive)
  2. 2Admin creates a coupon — assigns it to the influencer with a discount % and commission %
  3. 3Influencer shares their code — their followers enter it at checkout for a discount
  4. 4System tracks usage — every use is recorded: how many times used, total discount given, commissions earned
  5. 5Influencer views own stats — logged-in influencers see their coupon performance in their account panel

Coupon Fields

FieldDescription
Coupon CodeUnique alphanumeric code the influencer shares (e.g. ANNA20)
Discount %Percentage off the cart total for the customer
Commission %Percentage of sale value owed to the influencer
Expiry DateOptional. Coupon automatically deactivates after this date.
StatusActive / Inactive — can be toggled without deleting
Used CountRead-only. Shows total number of times the coupon was redeemed.

Business value: Instead of paying flat influencer fees, you only pay commission on actual sales driven — making this a performance-based marketing channel with zero upfront cost risk.

Section 09

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use my own domain and branding?

Yes. The platform is white-label. Your logo, brand colors, hero images, and store name are all configured per tenant. No code changes needed.

How many products can I have?

There is no built-in product limit. The catalog scales with your database and server resources.

Do customers need an account to buy?

An account is required to complete checkout — this enables order tracking, address management, and secure purchase history.

Can I have products with different sizes or colors?

Yes. The variant system allows unlimited attribute combinations (e.g. Size: S/M/L, Color: Red/Blue). Each variant has its own stock quantity.

How does the influencer coupon system work?

You create an influencer profile, then assign them a coupon code with a discount % for customers and a commission % for yourself to track. The system counts every redemption automatically.

Is the store mobile-friendly?

Yes. The entire storefront is built mobile-first with touch gestures, swipe navigation, a dedicated bottom navigation bar, and images optimized for all screen sizes.

What happens if a payment fails or is cancelled?

The customer is redirected to a cancellation page. The cart is preserved and they can try again. No order is recorded for failed payments.

Can I run multiple stores from one codebase?

Yes. The multi-tenant architecture means you deploy the same codebase with a different TENANT environment variable per store. Each store has its own configuration, branding, and data.
